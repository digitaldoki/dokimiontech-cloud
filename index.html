FAQ

Is this a Microsoft license resale?

No. You’re accessing a secure, cloud‑hosted desktop that we manage. Software is licensed to DigitalDokimion and used to provide a hosted service, not redistributed to end users.

Can I install apps on my own device?

No installs are required. Everything runs in your browser via your cloud desktop. Export your work to OneDrive, Dropbox, or local storage.

How do renewals and expirations work?

Before your term ends, we email reminders. If you don’t renew, your desktop is deprovisioned and reset. Always back up your files to your storage.
